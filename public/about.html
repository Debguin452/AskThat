What is whoispr?

An anonymous Q&A platform built for honest, real conversations without the awkwardness.

The idea

whoispr started with a simple question: what would people say to you if they knew you would never find out it was them? Turns out — quite a lot. And most of it is actually pretty meaningful.

We built whoispr to give people a safe, clean space to share honest thoughts, ask genuine questions, and say things they might not feel comfortable saying out loud. No accounts, no tracking, no theatre.

How it works

You create a personal link with your username. Anyone who visits that link can send you an anonymous message — a question, a compliment, something they've always wanted to tell you. You read those messages on your private dashboard. Messages expire after 48 hours automatically.

Senders choose to remain anonymous. The platform never reveals who sent what. Not to you, not to anyone.

Our values

Anonymity by design

We do not store sender identity. The architecture makes it technically impossible to link a message to a person.

No spam, no noise

Automated filtering keeps your inbox full of real messages. Junk does not make it through.

No accounts ever

No email, no password, no data collection. Your username is your entire identity on the platform.

Honesty by default

We believe people have more to say when the stakes feel lower. We give them the space to say it.
